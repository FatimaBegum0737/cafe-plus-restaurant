Our Story

Three Generations of
Royal Hospitality

Royal Cafe & Restaurant began in 2005 as a single candlelit table in the heart of the city — a humble promise to treat every guest like family, and every dish like an heirloom recipe worth perfecting.

Today, that promise has grown into a celebrated address for refined dining, where our chefs blend time-honoured technique with modern artistry, and every plate is finished with the same devotion as our very first.
